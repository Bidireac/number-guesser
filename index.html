Roll The Dice To Start The Game

You

Computer

In order to play this game, first you'll have to roll the dice. If you win the first round, you get more tries, 30 to be precisely, and 15 if you lose. Your number of tries is randomly generated. After that, you can adjust your number guesser by using the arrows, left or right (keyboard included), to increase or decrease the number from your last guess.
